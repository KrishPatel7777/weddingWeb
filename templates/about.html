Who We Are

Parampara Wedding Planner is your trusted companion in celebrating life's most precious moments with grace, tradition, and authenticity. We are storytellers of culture, architects of memories, and guardians of timeless Indian wedding traditions.

Our Story

Parampara, meaning "tradition" in Sanskrit, was born from a profound respect for the rich cultural heritage that defines Indian weddings. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to preserve and celebrate the sacred rituals and customs that have been passed down through generations.

Founded by passionate individuals who witnessed the beauty of traditional ceremonies blending with contemporary celebrations, we recognized the need for wedding planners who truly understand the soul of Indian culture. Each ceremony holds deep significance, every ritual tells a story, and we are honored to be the custodians of these precious moments.

Today, Parampara stands as a bridge between ancestral wisdom and modern elegance, ensuring that your wedding day honors both your roots and your dreams.
